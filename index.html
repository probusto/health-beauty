Healthy soups

When the weather cools down and you're in the mood for cozy comfort food, a warm bowl of soup is always a good option to warm you up. So the next time the temperatures drop, whip up one of these easy healthy soup recipes that are both good and good for you. There are nutritious versions of all your favorite bowls on this list, including pumpkin soups, chicken noodle soups, and butternut squash soups. Besides all the classics, there are a ton of other savory ideas on this list, including a pesto, chicken, and kale white bean soup, a lemon chicken cauliflower soup, and a sausage and tortellini soup. If you're a vegetarian or a vegan, you'll be thrilled with this roundup, because there are plenty of meatless soups on this list. The cauliflower bisque with brown butter croutons, roasted garlic and asparagus soup, and cilantro and sweet corn soup are all so delicious, even meat eaters will enjoy them. Along with being nutritious, many of these recipes are incredibly easy to prepare. Some can be made right in your slow cooker or your Instant Pot, which means you don't have to do any cooking at all. There are also several healthy soup recipes that are made on the stovetop (and are ready in as little as 15 minutes), which makes them ideal for busy weeknights.
